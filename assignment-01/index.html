Aryan Khatwad's Top 5 Movies

  1. 1. The Dark Knight

    Batman, the hero of Gotham City, faces off against the Joker, an anarchistic criminal who wants to see how far Batman can go to save Gotham.

  2. 2. Interstellar

    Set in a future where Earth is suffering from famine, a group of astronauts travel through space in search of a new home for humanity

  3. 3. Inception

    A professional thief is hired to infiltrate the subconscious of a target via dream-sharing technology.

  4. 4. Memento

    A man with anterograde amnesia uses a system of tattoos, photographs, and notes to find the man who killed his wife and caused his condition.

  5. 5. Oppenheimer

    A biopic following the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer, who led the Manhattan Project that developed the first nuclear weapons.
